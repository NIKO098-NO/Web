About Atlanta City Roleplay

Atlanta City Roleplay is a server founded on January 25, 2026, and its Board of Founders is a community-driven ERLC server inspired by the vibrant State of Georgia. The server focuses on realistic and structured roleplay, enforcing high standards of roleplay to keep scenarios immersive and fair. Players can take on roles within Georgia State Patrol, Fulton County Sheriffs Office, Atlanta Police Department, Atlanta Fire Department, Georgia Department of Transportation, or Civilian Life, with regular events and community-driven scenarios bringing the city to life. Built on respect, inclusivity, and authenticity, Atlanta City Roleplay offers its members a professional yet enjoyable environment to experience serious roleplay and connect with others.
